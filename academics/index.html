Academic Excellence

A comprehensive Nigerian curriculum designed to build independent thinkers, confident learners, and future high-achievers.

Nursery School (Early Years)

The early years are the most critical stage of a child’s development. In our Nursery section, we focus on learning through play, exploration, and structured activities. Our goal is to build your child's confidence, motor skills, and love for school in a warm and colorful environment.

What They Learn:

  • Phonics & Letter Recognition
  • Basic Numeracy (Number Work)
  • Handwriting Readiness
  • Rhymes & Expressive Arts
  • Social Habits & Etiquette

Primary School (Basic 1 – 6)

Our Primary curriculum is designed to give pupils a solid foundation in literacy, numeracy, and general knowledge. We challenge our students to think critically and ask questions. By Primary 5 and 6, pupils are rigorously prepared for their National Common Entrance Examinations to ensure a seamless transition into top secondary schools.

Core Subjects Include:

  • Mathematics
  • English Language & Diction
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • National Values (Civic Ed.)
  • Quantitative & Verbal Reasoning
  • Computer Studies (ICT)

Junior Secondary School (JSS 1 – 3)

The JSS level bridges the gap between basic education and specialized senior secondary subjects. Here, students are introduced to a broader range of subjects that test their analytical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. This phase culminates in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) / Junior WAEC.

Core Areas of Study:

  • Mathematics & English
  • Basic Science & Tech (BST)
  • Pre-Vocational Studies
  • Business Studies
  • National Values Education
  • Cultural & Creative Arts

Preparing for Future WAEC & NECO Success

While Senior Secondary Certificate Examinations (SSCE) like WAEC and NECO are written in SS3, the preparation starts here at Jonoch Academy. A house is only as strong as its foundation.

By enforcing a strict culture of independent studying, conducting continuous assessments, and discouraging all forms of examination malpractice, we equip our JSS3 graduates with the exact study habits, analytical skills, and academic confidence they need to comfortably pass their future WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations in flying colors.
