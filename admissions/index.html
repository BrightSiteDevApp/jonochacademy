Admissions

Join the Jonoch Academy Family. Here is everything you need to know about enrolling your child for the new academic term.

How to Apply in 4 Easy Steps

We have made our admission process as simple and transparent as possible for parents.

1

Obtain the Admission Form

Visit the school premises to purchase the admission form, or download it directly from our website and print it out.

2

Submit the Completed Form

Fill out the form with accurate details and return it to the school administrative office along with the required documents listed below.

3

Attend the Entrance Examination

Your child will be scheduled for a brief entrance examination or oral interview (depending on their class level) to help us place them appropriately.

4

Receive Admission Letter & Pay Fees

Successful candidates will be issued an admission letter. Simply pay the school fees into the designated bank account to secure your child's space!

Required Documents

Please bring photocopies of the following documents when submitting your child's form:

  • Two (2) recent passport-sized photographs of the child.
  • A photocopy of the child's Birth Certificate.
  • Last academic term's report card (for pupils transferring from another school).
  • Immunization record (for Nursery pupils only).

About the Entrance Examination

Nursery Applicants: No written exams. We simply conduct a friendly oral interaction and play observation to assess their readiness.

Primary & JSS Applicants: Candidates will be tested on basic Mathematics, English Language, and General Knowledge based on their previous class level. This simply helps us know their academic strengths and where they need the most help.

Have Questions About Admissions?

Our admin team is always happy to assist you with the process.
