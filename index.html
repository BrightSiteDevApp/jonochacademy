Why Choose Jonoch Academy?

Choosing the right school for your child is one of the most important decisions you will make. Here is why we are the right choice for your family:

Strong Academic Foundation

Our curriculum is structured to help students excel in standard examinations (including BECE) and prepare them for future academic success.

Small Class Sizes

We keep classes small to ensure our dedicated teachers give personalized attention to every student, ensuring no child is left behind.

Strict Moral Discipline

We instill strong moral values, respect, and discipline, raising our students to be responsible members of society.

Safe Environment

Your child's safety is our priority. Our premises are well-secured, clean, and designed to make learning comfortable and enjoyable.

Our School Sections

Nursery School

A colorful, engaging, and play-based environment where children develop basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills under patient, experienced educators.

Primary School

Focusing on building independent thinkers with a robust blend of subjects that encourage reading, writing, and mathematical problem-solving.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1 – 3)

Bridging the gap between foundational learning and advanced studies, equipping students thoroughly for their Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).
